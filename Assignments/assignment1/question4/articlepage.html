Virat Kohli – Biography & Top Records

Born: November 5, 1988 | Nationality: Indian

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest modern-day cricketers, known for his aggressive batting style, unmatched consistency, and leadership qualities. He has been a pillar of the Indian cricket team for more than a decade, dominating all formats of the game.

His incredible work ethic, passion for fitness, and never-give-up attitude have made him a role model for millions. Kohli has broken numerous world records and continues to be one of the most influential sportspersons globally.

Top 3 Records

  • Most ODI Centuries in the World – 50+
    Virat Kohli surpassed Sachin Tendulkar's long-standing record, becoming the player with the most ODI hundreds.

  • Fastest to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000 & 11,000 ODI Runs
    He holds multiple “fastest to” batting milestones, showcasing his unbelievable consistency.

  • Over 25,000 International Runs
    Kohli is among the few players in cricket history to cross this massive milestone.
